Andrei Ivanov (Russian: Андрей Вячеславович Иванов; born on 24 December 1971 in Tallinn) is an Estonian-Russian writer.[1]

He graduated from Tallinn Pedagogical University, studying Russian philology. After graduating, he lived many years in Scandinavian countries. At the beginning of 2000s, he returned to Estonia. Since then, he is focused on writing. Since 2013 he is a member of Estonian Writers' Union.[1]
