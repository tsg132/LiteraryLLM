Vasily Yan (Russian: Василий Ян; 4 January 1875 [O.S. 23 December 1874], Kiev, Russian Empire – August 5, 1954, Zvenigorod, Moscow Oblast), also spelled "Vassily Yan" (or "Ian") or just "V. Yan", was the pen name of Vasily Grigoryevich Yanchevetsky (Russian: Василий Григорьевич Янчевецкий), a Russian and Soviet writer, author of famous historic novels.[1][2]
