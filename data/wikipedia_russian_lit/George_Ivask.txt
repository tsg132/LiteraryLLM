George Ivask (Russian: Yuri Pavlovich Ivask, Юрий Павлович Иваск,  Estonian: Jüri Ivask; September 14, 1907 – February 13, 1986) was a Russian poet and literary critic; in his later years he was an American scholar of Russian literature.[1] Ivask was born to a family of an Estonian-German father and Russian mother and he identified culturally as a Russian.[2]
